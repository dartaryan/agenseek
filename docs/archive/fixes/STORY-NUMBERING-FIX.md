## Story Numbering Convention

### Regular Epic Stories
Format: `[Epic].[Story]` (e.g., 5.1, 7.4)

### On-the-Go Stories
Format: `0.[Number]` where:
- **0.1-0.5:** Initial on-the-go enhancements
- **0.6:** Dark Mode (postponed)
- **0.7-0.9:** Avatar, Admin, Settings improvements
- **0.10.1-0.10.3:** Journey system (divided from 0.10)
- **0.11-0.13:** Cleanup and organization
- **0.14:** Avatar completion
- **0.15:** Dark Mode polish
- **0.16+:** Additional on-the-go enhancements

Numbers may skip or be non-sequential based on when issues arise.
